Welcome to my internet home, please feel free to have a look around. Use the menu on the left to navigate. Some of the pages have information about my professional work: if you wish to hire me as a solo or collaborative pianist or harpsichordist, the About, Events/Blog, Videos, Projects, and Repertoire pages may be of some interest. If you wish to commission a composition or arrangement, or hire me as a typesetter, then you can look at all the pages under the Music heading to explore my previous work. You can then get in touch with me via the Contact page - I welcome all enquiries, professional or just comments or questions.

Otherwise, this site is essentially a repository of things that come from my brain. The Art page is a portal to my visual art, you can explore all the music I have written over the past twenty-five years, as well as the most important performance projects I've been involved in over that time. In particular, the 2011-2022 (TreeKnot) page is an ambitious attempt to bring together 37 works written largely in my twenties into an explorable graph of musical structure, integrating it with artwork drawn especially to illuminate the inner structure of the music itself.

The vast bulk of this material is offered for free, under a Creative Commons license. Please head to the Support page to view my philosophy on patronage and copyright in the digital age, and please consider helping keep my creative artistic practice sustainable.

I hope you enjoy your time spent here :)

-PdeJ.
